## 👨‍💻 About Me

🧠 I build products **end-to-end** — from feature scoping to deployment and iteration — across hiring tech, sports analytics, trading-strategy backtesting, and real-estate lead-gen.

⚙️ My stack is **React, Next.js, Node.js, and FastAPI** up front with **PostgreSQL and ETL pipelines** underneath. At Insight Fusion Analytics, the products I ship serve **1K+ daily active users** across 3 product lines.

🎯 **Product-oriented by default** — I think in user problems, funnels, and outcomes. That instinct is sharpened by **16 strategic product teardowns** (Netflix, Uber, Spotify &amp; more) and an **IBM Product Management** certificate.

🏒 Off the keyboard, I **lead a team of match analysts** at **Hudl (Instat)**, turning 50+ metrics per ice-hockey game into insight for professional teams and coaches.
